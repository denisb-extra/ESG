OUR MISSION

In today's world, the unexpected is the new normal. Nations face an array of asymmetric threats that endanger their security and stability. Unlike conventional threats, these come from smaller-scale adversaries with limited resources, making them difficult to detect and eliminate using traditional military means. The capacity to withstand and overcome these challenges can only be built using specialized engineering solutions.

That’s why we at ESG (Engineering Solutions Group) have made it our mission to empower the resilience and readiness of nations and military forces by delivering unmatched integrated defense and security engineering solutions.

WHO WE ARE

ESG is a leading defense engineering company delivering innovative technologies, high-quality engineering products, and solutions to the defense and security markets.

Our solutions have been rigorously proven in action and tailored to the client's needs - encompassing three key areas: unit building and projects (Tactical Operations), professional defense engineering training, and innovative technologies integration.

LEADERSHIP TEAM

Our team is made up of highly skilled professionals, former officers of the Elite Special Operations Engineering Unit of the IDF. We bring extensive expertise in Explosive Ordnance Disposal (EOD), IED Detection and Mitigation, Explosion and Ballistic Protection, Demolition and Disarmament, as well as other explosive-related fields.

PROJECTS

At ESG, we specialize in leading large-scale projects both in Israel and worldwide. Our projects range from establishing ammunition dismantling lines to clearing minefields, destroying and disposing of ammunition, leading activities in the underground medium, and protecting sensitive facilities. We are proud to be operating worldwide at the highest international standards and having built up defense engineering units at all levels, from full-scale engineering corps to specific units, including regiments, special units and unique units dedicated to handling unconventional terror and IED threats.

TRAINING

ESG provides specialized training to enhance the expertise of defense engineering personnel. Our instructors are combat-experienced military engineering experts who train, build, and support professional organizations around the world. Our training covers all areas of defense engineering, including demining, C.I.E.D., breaching, E.O.D., underground medium, and N.B.C. We are authorized worldwide to provide professional engineering training under standards required for the operation of civil and private organizations.
